What is Camera Person NFT?

Camera Person NFT is a collection of tokens on the ethereum blockchain that brings members of the photography community together through graphic art that represents photographers in the NFT space. The main goal is to collectively put out group photography projects that combine the fun of larger scale collectable pfp projects with the beauty in the art of photography and assembling collections.


Community Photography Collection?

Owning one Camera Person NFT allows one photograph entry into each of our community photography collections.

100% of our community photography collections proceeds go to the artists that produced the work for the collection.

Only one submission is allowed per Creator although those who own 2+ Camera Person NFTs will be able to register another Creator + that creators' wallet to contribute to the collection (via cameraperson.xyz).

The cameraperson.xyz dApp allows creators to register as Creators in our community photography collections via the Camera Person NFTs in their wallets. Creators can submit descriptions/titles of their photographs and also attribute specific traits to their photos(focal length, location, etc.) which will be expressed in the metadata of the NFTs in the final photography collection.

Creators who contribute to the collection will keep 100% of the mint proceeds from their photograph. They will also keep 100% of the royalties collected via marketplace platforms on their photograph if it sells on the secondary market after initial mint.

Every single piece submitted will be included in the collection. No gatekeeping. This is a great opportunity to onboard friends into the NFT space, who have maybe never minted a piece before or are just hesitant to jump in.This is a unique experiment in the NFT space - the first Community Photography Collection of its kind- that unites creators incentives in working on a photography project that doesn't set boundaries to those who would like to participate.

Our dApp is currently hidden on this site as we just recently released a collection - it will be back up soon to prepare for future collections.

Submissions for our first collection ended on June 6th 2022 and began minting on June 20th 2022 @ noon est.

The body of work includes 122 photographs produced by 122 artists with work from 39 different countries.

Each piece costs .05 ETH

100% of the funds from mint go to the artists that produced the work in the collection once it is fully minted out or if the community votes to recieve the payments in portions (once voted on- not passed).
